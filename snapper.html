How to Use Snapper

Snapper is the easiest way to pay your fare. You can order a Snapper Card online from
The Snapper Store

Touch the card to the reader at the front of the bus to tag on

The reader displays your current balance and your fare
A green circle means you have tagged on

A red cross means something went wrong. The reader will say 'Please Try Again'
or 'Please Pay The Driver'

Touch your card to the reader at the front or back of the bus to tag off and disembark carefully
